GRAPHIC DESIGN

THE FISH

    An animal project graphic design that I have idea from koi fish. You can see the light in between the picture because I want to make it have a deep dimension. Black side is for the dark, where they cannot reach the light. And with the white part represents for the part when it has light directly. The head of the fish is made by a lot of curve and have the spiral in here is the eyes.

    The small details stay separately with the main part is bubble in the water.

    In the overall, The Fish is circle form. It's means whole detail is contains in circle.



THE CAT

    I don't know but when I make a koi fish, this sketch I have in draft at first time with koi fish. I decided to do it because my teacher said it has really good shape and contrast. I think about black cat, which is character in famous anime. And at last, the moon layer is put on for making light crossing. Cat-moon always go together. And the huge moon become small represents for timing.

    Like The Fish, The Cat is circle form.



TRICERATOP

    This is extra work for animal project graphic design because I love to do something cool, so I think about doing a dinosaur. I like Triceratops because it's looked strong, and in some way, it has a lot of detail in body like horn, nose, a fan after head. Although it's looked strong, it's herbivore.

    With Triceratops, I made it follow the tattoo design depend on the detail of the skull more than the skin.

    Triceratops also is circle form.
